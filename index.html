Pablo Muñoz Suárez

I am currently a PhD researcher in the IdCONTROL project, part of the MR-DYNAMO Austrian-funded initiative. Since October 2025, I have been working with Barbara Kaltenbacher at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Klagenfurt.

In September 2024, I defended my master's thesis, “Finite element approximation for a convective Brinkman--Forchheimer problem coupled with a heat equation”, at the Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (UTFSM), under the supervision of Enrique Otarola and Gilberto Campana.

In January 2023, I defended my thesis in Mathematical Civil Engineering, titled “Análisis de bifurcaciones para modelos dinámicos en redes neuronales excitatorias-inhibitorias bajo la presencia de sinapsis electroquímica,” under the supervision of Pablo Aguirre and Patricio Orio.
